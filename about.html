About Demon Slayer:

Demon Slayer follows the journey of Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy whose family is brutally slaughtered by demons. Determined to avenge their deaths and find a cure for his demon-turned sister, Nezuko, Tanjiro becomes a member of the Demon Slayer Corps, battling powerful demons and unraveling the mysteries of his own bloodline in a quest to save humanity.


Synopsis:

After his family is tragically slaughtered by demons, Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted and skilled young man, embarks on a journey of revenge and redemption. He joins the elite Demon Slayer Corps, a group of warriors trained to battle the demonic creatures threatening humanity. Alongside his sister, Nezuko, who has been transformed into a demon herself, Tanjiro must face unimaginable challenges, unravel the mysteries of his own bloodline, and ultimately find a cure for Nezuko's condition. With his unique water-breathing technique and unwavering determination, Tanjiro fights to restore peace and vanquish the forces of darkness.
