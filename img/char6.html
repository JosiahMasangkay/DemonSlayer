Kyojuro Rengoku

Kyojuro is a Flame Hashira, one of the elite members of the Demon Slayer Corps. He is characterized by his vibrant personality, unwavering sense of justice, and his mastery of the Flame Breathing technique. Kyojuro plays a significant role in the "Mugen Train" arc and leaves a lasting impact on the story.
