Nezuko Kamado

Tanjiro's younger sister, Nezuko was turned into a demon during the attack on their family. Despite her transformation, she retains her humanity and displays protective instincts towards her brother. Nezuko possesses unique demon abilities, such as size manipulation and blood demon art, which she uses to aid Tanjiro in their battles against demons.
